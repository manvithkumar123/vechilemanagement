Traffic Rules & Regulations

Following traffic rules is essential for road safety. Here are some important traffic signs and rules to remember:

Important Traffic Signs

Stop Sign

Come to a complete stop and proceed only when it's safe.

Yield Sign

Slow down and yield to traffic that has the right-of-way.

Speed Limit

Drive within the posted speed limit for safety.

No Entry

Do not enter this road or area from this direction.

No Parking

Parking is not allowed in this area.

Pedestrian Crossing

Watch for pedestrians crossing the road at this location.

Key Traffic Rules

  • Always wear seatbelts

    Driver and all passengers must wear seatbelts at all times.

  • Follow speed limits

    Adhere to posted speed limits to ensure safety of all road users.

  • No drunk driving

    Never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

  • No mobile phones while driving

    Using mobile phones while driving is prohibited.

  • Yield to emergency vehicles

    Always give way to ambulances, fire engines, and police vehicles.

  • Use indicators for turns

    Always signal your intention to turn using indicators.
